# Chinese Graded Reader Stories - Complete Collection

- ~500 Chinese characters per story
- Word-by-word segmentation with pinyin and English translations
- Genre diversity (Sci-fi, Historical Fiction, Magical Realism, Comedy, Mystery, Drama, Slice of Life, Philosophical)
- Vocabulary appropriate for each HSK level

## HSK 3 Stories (5 stories - detailed outlines)

### 1. 标签眼镜 (The Label Glasses) - Sci-fi
**Concept**: Special glasses show floating labels above objects revealing what they're "thinking"
- Old tree: "我见过三代人" (I've seen three generations)
- Bicycle: "主人从来不给我上油" (Owner never oils me)
- Coffee cup: "我更喜欢茶" (I prefer tea)
- Narrator realizes everything has a story and history
- Learns to appreciate ordinary objects more
- **Key vocabulary**: 眼镜 (glasses), 标签 (label), 想法 (thoughts), 物品 (objects), 历史 (history), 发现 (discover), 普通 (ordinary), 特别 (special)

### 2. 进城 - 1985 (Moving to the City - 1985) - Historical Fiction
**Concept**: Young person's first time arriving in Reform-era Beijing
- Takes train from countryside with small luggage
- Overwhelmed by tall buildings, crowds, bicycles everywhere
- Finds small room to rent, starts factory job
- Writes letters home describing new modern life
- Contrast between rural past and urban future
- **Key vocabulary**: 城市 (city), 农村 (countryside), 火车 (train), 工厂 (factory), 改革 (reform), 年代 (era), 生活 (life), 变化 (change), 适应 (adapt)

### 3. 诚实的自行车 (The Honest Bicycle) - Magical Realism
**Concept**: Magical bicycle only moves when rider tells it true stories
- Protagonist tries lying to make stories more interesting - bike stops
- Must share real memories, honest feelings to keep riding
- Learns the value of truth through conversations with the bike
- Bike becomes therapist/friend through this unique relationship
- **Key vocabulary**: 自行车 (bicycle), 诚实 (honest), 故事 (story), 真实 (true), 谎言 (lie), 移动 (move), 记忆 (memory), 分享 (share)

### 4. 错误的手机 (Wrong Phone) - Comedy
**Concept**: Two people with same name accidentally swap identical phones
- Both named 李明 (Li Ming), both have same phone model
- Receive each other's calls and messages causing hilarious confusion
- One is doctor, one is chef - opposite life situations
- Coordinate complex swap back while living each other's lives briefly
- Become friends after the mix-up is resolved
- **Key vocabulary**: 手机 (phone), 错误 (mistake), 交换 (exchange), 信息 (message), 混乱 (confusion), 同名 (same name), 解决 (solve)

### 5. 阳台花园 (The Balcony Garden) - Drama
**Concept**: Elderly neighbor teaches protagonist about gardening and life lessons
- Young person stressed about career moves in next door
- Elderly neighbor invites them to help with balcony garden
- Through seasons of planting, watering, waiting for growth
- Learns patience, the value of slow growth, finding peace
- Neighbor shares wisdom from long life through gardening metaphors
- **Key vocabulary**: 阳台 (balcony), 花园 (garden), 种 (plant), 邻居 (neighbor), 耐心 (patience), 成长 (grow), 智慧 (wisdom), 生命 (life)

## HSK 4 Stories (5 stories - detailed outlines)

### 1. 明天的茶 (Tea from Tomorrow) - Sci-fi
**Concept**: Special tea brewing method reveals alternate life paths
- Antique teapot shows visions of "what if" scenarios while drinking
- See alternate life where different career choice was made
- See alternate timeline where stayed in hometown vs. moved to city
- Each cup shows a different possible past/future decision
- Realizes current life, despite imperfections, is valuable
- **Key vocabulary**: 茶 (tea), 未来 (future), 选择 (choice), 命运 (fate), 可能性 (possibility), 后悔 (regret), 珍惜 (cherish), 人生 (life path)

### 2. 书法家的选择 (The Calligrapher's Choice) - Historical Fiction
**Concept**: Song Dynasty calligraphy master torn between artistic integrity and money
- Wealthy merchant offers fortune for copying classical works to sell as antiques
- Would provide family security but compromise artistic honor
- vs. continuing honest work teaching poor students
- Decides to refuse money, continue teaching authentic art
- Students' success becomes his true legacy
- **Key vocabulary**: 书法 (calligraphy), 艺术家 (artist), 选择 (choice), 诚实 (integrity), 金钱 (money), 名誉 (reputation), 传统 (tradition), 学生 (students), 朝代 (dynasty)

### 3. 记忆茶馆 (The Memory Teahouse) - Magical Realism
**Concept**: Mysterious teahouse where each tea variety changes a specific memory
- Jasmine tea: makes painful memory less sharp
- Oolong: brightens happy memory to vivid detail
- Pu'er: shows memory from another person's perspective
- Protagonist learns memories shape identity, both joy and pain needed
- Some memories better left unchanged to preserve who we are
- **Key vocabulary**: 记忆 (memory), 茶馆 (teahouse), 改变 (change), 过去 (past), 情感 (emotion), 忘记 (forget), 珍贵 (precious), 身份 (identity)

### 4. 奶奶玩网络 (Grandma Goes Digital) - Comedy
**Concept**: 70-year-old grandmother tries online matchmaking with hilarious results
- Grandchildren teach her to use smartphone and dating apps
- Her honest, direct messages shock potential matches
- Accidentally becomes viral sensation for authentic posts
- Confusion over emojis, filters, modern dating slang
- Eventually finds friendship group online with other elderly people
- Teaches family that connection matters more than technology skills
- **Key vocabulary**: 奶奶 (grandma), 网络 (internet/online), 手机 (smartphone), 应用 (app), 约会 (dating), 交友 (make friends), 误会 (misunderstanding), 现代 (modern), 技术 (technology)

### 5. 公园观察者 (The Park Observer) - Philosophical
**Concept**: Writer finds inspiration watching ordinary moments in city park
- Daily observations: elderly doing tai chi, mother with toddler, chess players
- Each person has invisible story, dreams, struggles
- Learns to see profound meaning in simple daily routines
- Small acts of kindness ripple through community
- Realizes greatest stories are all around us in normal life
- Writes book about the park that becomes unexpectedly popular
- **Key vocabulary**: 公园 (park), 观察 (observe), 日常 (daily life), 普通 (ordinary), 意义 (meaning), 灵感 (inspiration), 深刻 (profound), 社会 (society), 哲学 (philosophy)

## Technical Specifications

### JSON Structure Format
Each story follows this structure:
```json
{
  "id": "hsk{level}_{genre}_{title_english}",
  "title": "中文标题",
  "english_title": "English Title",
  "genre": "Genre Name",
  "hsk_level": 1,
  "word_count": 500,
  "text": [
    {
      "word": "我",
      "pinyin": "wǒ",
      "translation": "I"
    }
  ]
}
```

### Vocabulary Guidelines by Level
- **HSK 1**: ~150 words (basic pronouns, simple verbs, common nouns)
- **HSK 2**: ~300 words (time words, more verbs, basic connectors)
- **HSK 3**: ~600 words (complex sentences, descriptive language)
- **HSK 4**: ~1200 words (abstract concepts, idiomatic expressions)
